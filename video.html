6 illustration videos that I animated for a Stanford Medical School research study will be posted once the study has been completed. (Below is a screenshot for reference of illustration style).

The below two videos were produced for Concept Studio: Space and Time.

"6 Days a Follower" is a video representing what it is like to be a YouTube "Beauty Guru" follower. For 6 days, I followed the makeup tutorial videos from different popular beauty gurus and copied their makeup in the context of my own room. This piece explores femininity and identity for the milennial woman.

"Farwell" is a video that sets out to systematically record the messy and emotional moment of a long distance couple saying farewell before being separated again. The system created for this video was that my partner and I were to sit and stare at each other, allowing whatever comes out to come out.
