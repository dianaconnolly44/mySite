Younger Art

From a young age, I have been creating art. This section is dedicated to the art that I either created on my own or completed during my sessions at the Interlochen Arts Academy summer programs. Before college, my primary interest was realistic portraits.

Imaging (60-160)

In CMU’s 2D Media Studio: Imaging course (60-160), taught by Professor Andrew Johnson, the class created artworks ranging from traditional drawing styles (e.g. figure sketches) to more exploratory formats (e.g. digitally generated images or collages). This page is dedicated to those pieces.
